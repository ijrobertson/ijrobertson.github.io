Top 10 Italian Shows to Watch

Watching TV in another language is one of the best ways to immerse yourself — and it's fun! Here are our 10 favourites:

  1. L'amica Geniale (My Brilliant Friend)
  2. L'allieva (The Pupil)
  3. Come Fai Sbagli (How to do Wrong)
  4. Un Medico in Famiglia (A Doctor in the Family)
  5. Summertime
  6. Zio Gianni (Uncle John)
  7. Luna Nera (Black Moon)
  8. Suburra: Blood on Rome
  9. Il Miracolo (The Miracle)
  10. Baby

Quick Tips (Suggerimenti)

Don't be afraid to make mistakes or have an accent — Italian is an expressive language, so lean into it! Speak slowly at first and keep practising.

How to Get Started (Come Iniziare)

  • Read a short story with your Lingua Bud.
  • Watch a TV show or movie in Italian with subtitles.
  • Explore our language charts to learn basic grammar and verbs. Learn Italian Basics
  • Challenge yourself with a short news article in Italian.
  • Travel to Italy and challenge yourself to speak only in Italian.
  • Practice every day — consistency is the fastest path to fluency.
