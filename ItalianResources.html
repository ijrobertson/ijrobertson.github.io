Tips (Suggerimenti)

Learning a language is hard. Don't be afraid to make mistakes. Don't be afraid if you have an accent. For many, having an accent is a sign of bravery - you put yourself out there to communicate with others!

Don't worry if you can't pronounce things perfectly right away. Try your best and remember that it's okay to speak slowly. Keep practicing and over time you will get better.

How to Get Started (Come Iniziare)

Not sure on how to get started with learning Italian? Don't worry, you're not alone! Try these suggestions below on how to get started with learning Italian:

  • Read a short story with your Lingua Bud.
  • Watch a tv show/movie in Italian with subtitles.
  • Explore our language charts to learn basic grammar and verbs. Click here to learn Italian Basics
  • Challenge yourself to read a short news article in Italian and ask your Lingua Bud to correct you on your errors.
  • Travel to Italy and challenge yourself to only speak in Italian.
  • Practice everyday! The fastest way to fluency is consistency.
